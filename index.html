Projects

The GC Wealth Project
The GC Wealth Project aims at expanding and consolidating access to the most up-to-date research and information on wealth, wealth inequalities, and wealth transfers and related tax policies, across countries and over time. I oversaw the development of the Wealth Topography database which included the conceptualization, designing the current data architecture, the national accounting mapping, and the automation of data gathering.
