I am a post-doctoral researcher at Roma Tre University within the Inequality in Rome Working Group and associated post-doctoral researcher with the The GC Wealth Project at CUNY.

My academic work examines the distributional implications of macroeconomic policy, with particular reference to monetary policy and household wealth.

Some of my writings have appeared in IRIS and Jacobin. Before my postdoc, I received a PhD in Economics (summa cum laude) from the Universities of Siena, Pisa and Florence.

For more details, check my CV or hit me up at rella.gia(at)gmail.com
